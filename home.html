WHAT PEOPLE SAY ABOUT US

Testimonies

"You cannot do this because you are a female" - Aba

"You must do this because you are a female ".The issue of gender roles and male dominance in prominent spaces always keeps my mind in space wondering what ever went wrong. I believe WE ALL CAN regardless of our gender because why not? I am a promulgator of the word to young girls to simply "Girl Up".

"I always fumbled with effective communication" - Jessica

As a member of the Education Committee that has become a thing of the past. I have learned how important it is to coordinate, be creative, and self-develop; especially since as an education committee member you are mostly holding the fort when it comes to public education. Thanks, M.Q Girl Up.

" Sexual and reproductive health were almost a taboo for discussion." - Anita

As young ones, only a few were priveleged to engage with experts on sexual health. Many of us resorted to the misleads of our inexperienced peers who seem to know more. With the enlightenment from M.Q, I want to be a force of change in this regard. I know I'd be totally up for this challenge
